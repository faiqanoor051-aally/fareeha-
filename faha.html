OUR STORY

Fareeha Noor

Born in Pakistan, inspired by grace — we create fashion that celebrates the modern Pakistani woman.

Our Vision

At Fareeha Noor, we believe every woman deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and uniquely herself. Our designs blend traditional Pakistani elegance with contemporary silhouettes.

Our Craft

Every garment is thoughtfully crafted using premium fabrics sourced from across Pakistan. From delicate lawn prints to rich formal wear, quality is never compromised.

Our Promise

Affordable luxury. We price our collections between Rs.1000–Rs.3000 so every woman can experience the elegance of Fareeha Noor without breaking the bank.

"Fashion is not just clothing — it is how you tell the world who you are."

Founded with Love in Sindh, Pakistan

Fareeha Noor was founded in 2020 by a passionate team of designers who grew up admiring the rich textile heritage of Pakistan. From the bustling markets of Sukkur to the fashion runways of Karachi, our brand brings that spirit of beauty to every Pakistani home. Today, we serve thousands of women across the country, delivering style, comfort, and elegance right to their doorstep.
